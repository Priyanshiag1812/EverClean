    About Us

    This is a common platform created to raise awareness about waste management issues and connect the community with relevant campagins and authorities. Such a platform can play a crucial role in addressing waste management challenges and fostering a sense of responsibilty among the community.

    Aim:To provide services to customer for effective waste management services to users

    Our project in terms of website will serve as such a platform which will focus on:

    • Educating Residents about waste management by displaying awareness campaigns, blogs to educate residents about the importance of proper waste disposal and the health risks associated with inadequate waste management, it will also act as an intermediate between general public for buying and selling of homemade materials made from waste.
    • Collaboration with Local Authorities: Work with local authorities to ensure proper waste collection and disposal services are available.
    • Garbage Bins Installation: Ensuring an adequate number of garbage bins are available to encourage residents to dispose of their waste properly.
